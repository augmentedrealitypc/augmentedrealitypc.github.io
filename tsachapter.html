OUR TSA CHAPTER

Panther Creek boasts of a wonderful TSA chapter. Despite the school being relatively young, PCHS has been successful throughout TSA. Just last year, our video game design team achieved 5th place at the national TSA conference. Recently, the PCHS robotics team has taken a regional championship win and qualifies for VEX states for the first time in school history. PCHS is also doing well in other events such as animatronics, engineering, biotechnology, and has added a good Scientific Visualization program and good computer programming classes. Recently, PCHS has added AP Computer Science and AP Computer Science Principles to its plethora of CTE Courses.

This year, the Panther Creek TSA is raising money for the American Cancer Society by selling Krispy Kreme doughnuts. All of the profit made will be donated to ACS.
